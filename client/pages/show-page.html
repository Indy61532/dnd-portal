Ashen Hydra

Ashen Hydra represents an extremely intelligent form of hydra that has adopted the instincts of dragons and the ability to absorb energy from molten lava. Its scales are an alloy of obsidian and dragon glass, granting it nearly impenetrable armor.

It often establishes its own territory in abandoned mining complexes, transforming them into massive incubators. In enclosed spaces it can recycle air using pyro-wind vortices, making movement difficult for opponents.

Each head controls a different stream of elemental energy and can coordinate them into devastating combinations. Experts recommend specialized parties with access to ice or gravitational magic.
